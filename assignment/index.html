Do You Know Where The Money Goes?

The Pareto Principle, which is also known as the 80-20 rule, can be applied in many fields, such as economics, science, software, and sport (Dunford, Su & Tamang, 2014). In economics, one popular phenomenon is that the richest 20% of the world's population generate 80% of the world's wealth.

Fortune 1000 in US are the selected top 1000 companies in America. Based on the Pareto Principle, most money are controlled by the few top companies. Therefore, by analysing the data from Fortune 1000, we can get an idea of where the main money goes in US.

This visualization presents Fortune 1000 data in US and the relation of the revenue of the companies in sectors and the gender of CEO. The ranking showed are based on the revenue of the company instead of profit.


An Overview of the Companies in Fortune 1000

This graph showed the rankings by revenue and sectors of overall Fortune 1000.

Interestingly, the revenue of the first company (Walmart) is almost two times of the revenue of the second company (CVS Health).

The number of companies shows in the graph can be selected by the slider below.


An Overview of the Sectors in Fortune 1000

This graph presented the total revenue of each sector.

The sum of the 5 highest sectors are much greater than the sum of the remainings, which means most incomes are made by Financials, Health Care, Retailing, Energy, and Technology in Fortune 1000.



A Map of the Companies in Fortune 1000

The below map presented the distribution of the companies in Fortune 1000.

One of the intersting points is that more companies in Fortune 1000 are located in the East America than located in the West. In addition, Financial is the highest number of companies in the East, whereas Technology is the largest number of companies in the West.



The gender of CEO in Fortune 1000

This graph showed the total revenue in sectors by the gender of CEO.

Male CEOs gain more revenue compared to female CEOs, especially in Financials. In addition, Health Care is the sector that female CEOs have the highest total revenue among all sectors.



Top 50 and others in Fortune 1000

This graph presented the total revenue of top 50 companies and the others in Fortune 1000.

Based on the Pareto Principle, most properties are generated by the vital few. In this donut chart, the vital few (Rank 1 - 50) have the revenue close to the others (Rank 51 - 1000). The total revenue is similar, but the number of companies is twenty times larger.



Conclusion

Generally speaking, from the data of Fortune 1000 in US, the highest total revenue is from the Financials sector, which is mostly found in the East America, the male CEO, and the top 50 companies. However, when it comes to the Rank 1 (Walmart), it is not in Financials and located in the Middle America. There are still other factors that influence the revenue of the companies. Therefore, we can keep finding where the money goes.
